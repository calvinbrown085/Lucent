What it does

Point Lucent at any HDHomeRun tuner on your network, pick a guide source, and you're watching in seconds.

Two guide sources

  • Gracenote — Enter a US postal code and Lucent fetches your local listings automatically.
  • XMLTV — Point Lucent at any self-hosted XMLTV URL. Streamed and cached on-device.

Built for tvOS 26

Native SwiftUI from the ground up. Liquid Glass lives on navigation, overlays, and settings — never on the content you're trying to watch.

Picture-in-Picture on iPhone and iPad

Keep watching while you message, browse, or scroll. PiP works directly on HDHomeRun's raw MPEG-TS streams.

Requirements

  • Apple TV with tvOS 26.2 or later, or iPhone/iPad on iOS 18+
  • HDHomeRun tuner on the same Wi-Fi/LAN
  • An antenna or compatible HDHomeRun input
  • US postal code for Gracenote, or any XMLTV feed for elsewhere

v1 scope

Lucent v1 is focused: live TV from your HDHomeRun, in your home, fast. No DVR, no series passes, no remote streaming, no accounts. Those can come later — the basics come first, and they come done right.
